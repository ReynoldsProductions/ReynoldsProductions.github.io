about

I am a filmmaker and live event producer with a history in web design and IT, I am applying for the position of Video and Livestream Producer at Github. I have worked in film and corporate production for fifteen years, for the last five years I have worked in the Bay Area with a focus on corporate communications for healthcare and tech companies throughout the valley. This has included everything from small one man band productions to large broadcast commercials, large scale live product announcements, and trade shows. A number of these projects have included internal live quarterly sales, earnings, and town hall meetings, over the last 11 months all of these projects have shifted to virtual online events and remote productions.

When I am not working I spend my time biking, beermaking, and DIYing anything I can dream up. Lately that has meant homelab server development for digital indepencence.
